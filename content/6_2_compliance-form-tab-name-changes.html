Compliance Form Tab Name Changes

The DOE/PNNL 90.1 Performance-based Compliance Form (the Compliance Form) undergoes continuous maintenance where updates are made based upon user feedback and when there are changes to 90.1 that impact the Compliance Form (i.e., the Compliance Form was updated to support 90.1 2022 in addition to 90.1 2016 and 2019 at the end of 2023). A few tab names have changed since previous versions of the Compliance Form. These changes are summarized below so that if an older version of the Compliance Form is being reviewed the reviewer can follow along with the review tips in the sections below.

  • Interior Lighting Summary tab (formerly called the Interior Lighting Model Inputs tab)
  • HVAC PRM tab (formerly called the Baseline HVAC App G tab)
  • Budget HVAC ECB tab (formerly called the Budget HVAC Section 11 tab)
  • Submittal+Mand. Req. Checklists (formerly called the Submittal Checklist tab)
